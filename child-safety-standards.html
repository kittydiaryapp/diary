Kitty Diary

Child Safety Standards

Kitty Diary is committed to protecting children and maintaining a safe environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM).

Overview

Kitty Diary is a social diary application that allows users to share personal experiences, photos, and connect with friends. While our app is designed for users 17 years of age and older, we recognize our responsibility to prevent and address any potential child safety concerns that may arise in social applications.

Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Kitty Diary has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation. We prohibit any content or behavior that sexually exploits, abuses, or endangers children, including grooming, sextortion, trafficking, or any form of sexual exploitation of minors.

Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) Policy

Kitty Diary strictly prohibits the creation, distribution, possession, or sharing of child sexual abuse material. Any CSAM content will be immediately removed and reported to law enforcement authorities.

Content Moderation and Safety Measures

Kitty Diary implements comprehensive content moderation systems to prevent and detect harmful content:

Automated Systems

  • AI-powered content filtering for inappropriate material
  • Image recognition technology to detect potential CSAM
  • Text analysis for grooming behavior and inappropriate language
  • Age verification systems to prevent underage users

Manual Review

  • 24/7 content moderation team
  • Expert review of flagged content
  • Regular audits of moderation systems
  • Training for moderators on CSAE and CSAM detection

In-App Reporting Mechanism

Kitty Diary provides in-app reporting mechanisms for users to report child safety concerns, inappropriate content, or suspicious behavior.

Legal Compliance

Kitty Diary complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations, including federal laws prohibiting CSAM and CSAE.

Prevention and Education

Kitty Diary actively works to prevent child safety issues through:

  • Regular safety awareness campaigns
  • Educational content about online safety
  • Partnerships with child safety organizations
  • Training for users on recognizing and reporting abuse
  • Age-appropriate content guidelines

Response Protocol

When child safety concerns are reported or discovered, Kitty Diary follows a strict response protocol:

  1. Immediate Action: Content is immediately removed and preserved for investigation
  2. Assessment: Trained safety team evaluates the report
  3. Law Enforcement: CSAM and serious CSAE cases are reported to authorities
  4. Account Action: Violating accounts are suspended or terminated
  5. Documentation: All actions are documented for legal purposes
  6. Follow-up: Ongoing monitoring and prevention measures

Policy Updates

Kitty Diary regularly reviews and updates our child safety standards to ensure they remain effective and compliant with evolving laws and best practices. Users will be notified of significant changes to these standards.
